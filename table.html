About scRNA-tools

The scRNA-tools database is a catalogue of software packages for analysing single-cell RNA sequencing data. It was created by the Oshlack Lab, a bioinformatics research group based in Melbourne, Australia and is now maintained at the Institute of Computational Biology in Munich, Germany and by a team of volunteers around the world.
